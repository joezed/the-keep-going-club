Who We Are

Keep Going Club is a grassroots mental health movement for anyone carrying quiet struggles behind a brave face. Born in Nottingham and led by people with lived experience, we create warm, creative spaces where it feels safe to talk, to be real, and to be held by community - without judgement, labels, or pressure.

We’re here for the humans behind the “I’m fine.”

Our Mission

To make early mental health support human, simple, and accessible - offering connection and care long before crisis point.
